## Tips for Good Prompts

1. **Be specific**: Include exact sizes, paths, or conditions
2. **State constraints**: Mention what you want to avoid or include
3. **Ask for explanations**: "Show me how to X and explain why it works"
4. **Iterate**: If first result isn't perfect, refine with "Can you modify this to..."
